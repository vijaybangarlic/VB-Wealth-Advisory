Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pause or alter my Mutual Fund SIP if I face a temporary cash crunch?
Yes, absolutely. Mutual Fund SIPs are fully flexible. You can pause, stop, or decrease your SIP investment amounts at any time without any financial penalty or loss of your existing accumulated capital.
Is a pre-policy health checkup mandatory for buying National Mediclaim policies?
For individuals up to 45 or 50 years of age with no prior medical history, medical check-ups are generally waived. For older clients or special high-value coverages, standard check-ups might be requested by the insurer.
What happens if I miss my LIC premium due date?
LIC provides a generous Grace Period of 30 days for quarterly, half-yearly, and annual payment modes (15 days for monthly mode). Your full risk cover remains active during this grace window.
